About

I'm a self-taught, front-end developer from Madison, WI - currently residing in Whitewater. I started learning web development because my grandpa needed a website to promote his Kentucky Bourbon Tours business, and haven't looked back since. Through my first year of experience, I've honed my skills in HTML and CSS, and began the pursuit of mastering Javascript.
